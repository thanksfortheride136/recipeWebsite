Macaroni And Cheese

Big Lou's Chicken will have you licking your lips.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts.
  • Olive Oil: 2 tablespoons to ensure the chicken is moist and gets a nice golden color.
  • Fresh Herbs: 2 tablespoons finely chopped (a mix of rosemary, thyme, and parsley works well).
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced, for added flavor.
  • Salt and Pepper: To taste, for seasoning.

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the olive oil, chopped herbs, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the herb mixture over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are well-coated.
  4. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and bake for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Once done, let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving. This ensures it remains juicy.
