Macaroni And Cheese

Big Lou's Macaroni and Cheese, this mac and cheese will have you coming back for more and more. Crafted with several different cheeses in order for maximum gooeyness.

Ingredients

  • BreadCrumbs - 12oz
  • Milk - 2 Cups
  • Macaroni - 1 Box
  • Shredded Cheese - 1 lb.
  • Condenses Milk - 1 Can

Directions

  1. Boil Macaroni
  2. Drain Macaroni
  3. Stir in Condensed Milk
  4. Mix in Shredded Cheese
  5. Stir Until Mixed
  6. Let rest for 1 minute and eat!
